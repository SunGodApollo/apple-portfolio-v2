About Me

Hello there! Welcome to my space. Let me tell you a bit about myself. I love cars, sunny weather, San Diego, the beach, videogames, and software development. The latter though, was not something I knew I loved, until I started doing it. It was always something that interested me but seemed extremely scary and daunting.

What I have come to love about software development is exactly this: the feeling of discomfort, excitement, the investigative aspect, if you will. I love the community, and how supportive everyone is of each other; knowing that I'm not alone in my software development journey has given me strength to keep going; to not give up.

I am so happy with my decision of becoming a software developer, and I am not looking back. Please check out the projects that I have developed and helped co-develop. I plan to add many more projects in the near future. My email, phone, and other links are also available. Please, give me a holler!
